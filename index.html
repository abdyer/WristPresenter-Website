A quick glance at your Pebble and you will instantly know what slide you are on and how many slides you have left. Change slides by pressing the top or bottom button on the right side of the Pebble.

Load presentations using iTunes USB File Sharing or from other iOS apps using the "Open In" menu.

View your presentations on a projector by mirroring your iPhone via Airplay or with a Lightning adapter.

  • Apple TV
  • Apple Lightning to VGA Adapter
  • Apple Lightning to HDMI Adapter
  • Reflector
  • Air Server

Travel light, leave your laptop at home. With just your iPhone, Pebble Smartwatch, and Wrist Presenter you can give great presentations easily.

Wrist Presenter requires an iPhone with iOS 7, a Pebble Smartwatch with version 2 software, and a PDF file.
