What file formats are supported?

Google Drive presentations are the only supported format.

Back to top

How do I load presentations?

The app connects to your Google Drive account(s) and displays all of your presentations.

Back to top

How do I view my presentations with a projector?

View your presentations on a projector with a Miracast dongle or an HDMI adapter on compatible devices. Chromecast support is planned for a future release.

Back to top

What smartwatches are supported?

The Pebble Smartwatch with version 2 software.

Back to top

How do I install the companion Pebble app?

On your phone or tablet, visit http://wristpresenter.com/watchapp/ in the web browser. You must have the Pebble Android app installed and a Pebble Smartwatch with version 2 software.

Back to top

How do I control presentations?

Change slides by pressing the top or bottom button on the right side of the Pebble.

Back to top

How do I exit presentations?

To exit a presentation, press the back button on your phone or tablet while viewing a presentation.
